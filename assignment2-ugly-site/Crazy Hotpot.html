I find hotpot foods particularly delightful in hot weather, despite their seemingly counterintuitive nature. Consuming a spicy broth leads to sweating, which, in turn, aids in cooling the skin. Subsequently, hydrating with ample water not only refreshes your mouth but also maintains proper hydration levels. So, when navigating the noisey streets of NYC during busy times, remember to treat yourself to a nourishing hotpot!
