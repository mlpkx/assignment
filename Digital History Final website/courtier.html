Courtiers

  • Minamoto no Shitagō 源順

    • Minamoto no Shitagō was a waka poet, scholar and nobleman.
    神代より色も変らぬ竹川のよよをば君ぞ数へ渡らむ (ceremonial)
    This poem was composed by him when he was at a featival held at the Saigū's residence at the Shosai'in (Hall of Initial Abstinence) in 976. This poem celebrates the longevity of the Saigū institution, which in turn symbolizes the prosperity and longevity of the Emperor's reign."
