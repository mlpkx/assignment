Poetry Divination

A Poem for Today

Curious about your fortune today? Poetry has the answer for you.
Welcome to the magical realm of poetry. I hail from 17th-century Japan, a Waka poet at heart. During a leisurely journey with friends to the town of Yoshino, I penned a poetic travelogue. It captures our emotions and observations along the way. As time has blessed it, this manuscript now possesses a unique magic, able to tell your luck for the day. Are you ready to embark on this magical journey? During this process, you will follow your own choices, and the poetic magic will reveal your fortune.
