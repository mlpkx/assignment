Poetry Divination

A Poem for Today

Curious about your fortune today? Let poetry reveal the answer. Welcome to the magical realm of poetry. I hail from 17th-century Japan, a Waka poet at heart.
During a leisurely journey with friends, I penned a poetic travelogue.
It captures the emotions and observations of our group along the way. As time has blessed it, this chronicle now possesses a unique magic, able to divine your destiny and fate for the day.
Are you ready to embark on this magical journey? During this process, you will make some choices, and the poetic magic will reveal your fortune and fate for today.
